Introduction

Reverse Stock Automation Bot (RAB) prioritizes the privacy and security of your information. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data and the measures we take to protect it. By using RAB, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Data Collection and Usage

RAB does not store any personal data or brokerage credentials. The brokerage credentials you provide are used exclusively by third-party APIs to facilitate the connection between your device and the brokerage endpoints. These third-party APIs also do not store your data.

Third-Party APIs

RAB uses the following third-party APIs to connect with various brokerage services:

  • Robinhood API by jmfernandes
  • Public API by NelsonDane
  • Webull API by tedchou12
  • Firstrade API by MaxxRK
  • Fennel API by NelsonDane

These APIs are solely used to establish a connection between your device and the brokerage services. They do not store your credentials or any personal data.

Security
